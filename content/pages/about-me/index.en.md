## Intro
Hi there! **TurobKre**'s here. Though you may see me around as **Kreee**, or **Kre³** for short. (I know, it’s a quirky name, right?).

For the past two years, I’ve been working in Marketing at a leading personal care company. In late 2025, I decided to start get serious about digital painting (self-taught). While I’m still very much in the "refining my technique" phase and my portfolio is just beginning to grow, my ambition is to become an amateur artist who does more than just paint: I want to build worlds. I’m passionate about using my illustrations to narrate the stories and characters living in my heart.
